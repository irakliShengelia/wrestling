SkyRunning Adventure

A high-altitude trail running camp across Georgia's majestic mountains

Apply Now

Overview

SkyRunning Adventure is a 5-day guided trail running experience across the Caucasus. Open to runners of all levels with strong fitness, this program blends endurance, exploration, and elevation in one unforgettable journey.

All accommodation, meals, gear transport, and local guides included. Limited to 16 participants.

What to Expect

Challenging Trails: Daily 15–20km trail runs at altitudes between 2000–3500m

Stunning Landscapes: Forests, ridgelines, alpine lakes, and hidden valleys

Expert Support: Professional trail guides and support vehicle access

Holistic Program: Includes mobility, breathwork, recovery, and evening talks

Community Vibe: Connect with others who thrive on challenge and nature
